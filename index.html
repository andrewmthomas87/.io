Meet .io

A program run by the Tech team in EPIC aiming to provide students an experience comparable to that of working in a software-oriented startup while in a forgiving environment with an emphasis on learning.

Why

  • Provides a "foot in the door" for students
  • Allows students to learn relevant technical and team skills in a forgiving and time-efficient environment
  • Allows for pursual of “dead-end” ideas, “clone” projects, and ideas not viable for an actual startup. These ideas often can be more educational for learning the skillset than pursuing something “real”.

How

  • Small teams of 3-5 students and a more experienced mentor pursue full-stack project MVPs (see Projects)
  • Project development organized using professional collaboration tools and Agile sprints and sprint review/planning meetings (see Schedule)
  • Weekly training meetings in lecture+interactive demo/problem style (see Curriculum)
  • Flexible drop-in work times in The Garage with available mentors
  • Demo day at end of quarter
